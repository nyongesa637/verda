## V. CONSTITUTIONAL FRAMEWORK

The petitioner relies, inter alia, on the following provisions of the
Constitution:

- Article 22 — every person has the right to institute court proceedings
  claiming that a right or fundamental freedom has been denied, violated,
  infringed or is threatened.
- Article 23 — jurisdiction of courts to uphold and enforce the Bill of Rights.
- Article 29 — freedom and security of the person.
- Article 33 — freedom of expression.
- Article 37 — freedom of assembly, demonstration, picketing and petition.
- Article 49 — rights of arrested persons.
- Article 50 — fair hearing.

## VI. PRAYER FOR RELIEFS

WHEREFORE the petitioner prays that this Honourable Court be pleased to
grant the following orders:

(a) A declaration that the arrest, detention and treatment of the
    petitioner(s) by the Respondents violated Articles 29, 33, 37, 49 and 50
    of the Constitution.
(b) A conservatory order compelling the immediate and unconditional release
    of the petitioner(s).
(c) An order of compensation under Article 23(3)(e) for the violations
    suffered.
(d) An order that the Respondents bear the costs of this petition.
(e) Any further or other relief this Honourable Court may deem just and
    expedient.
